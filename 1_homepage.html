What is Space Biology?

Space biology studies the effects of the space environment on biological organisms, from humans to plants and animals. It is essential for future space exploration and understanding fundamental biological processes.

Importance of Space Biology

Space biology research is crucial for ensuring the safety and health of astronauts, advancing medical knowledge, and developing sustainable life support systems for long-term space missions.
